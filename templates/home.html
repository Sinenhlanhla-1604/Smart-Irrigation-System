About Our System

Our smart irrigation system leverages affordable IoT sensors to monitor essential parameters such as soil moisture, tank levels, and rainfall in real time. By using Sigfox for reliable, low-energy communication, the system ensures consistent and timely data transmission, even in remote areas where conventional communication networks might fail. With its easy-to-use dashboard, the system empowers farmers to make informed decisions about water usage, reducing wastage and optimizing irrigation schedules. The sensors track moisture levels in the soil, ensuring that irrigation is only triggered when needed, preventing over-watering and conserving precious water resources. In addition to monitoring soil conditions, the system also keeps an eye on tank levels and rainfall. By detecting when water levels are high, it allows farmers to make the most of rainfall, storing excess water for future use. The system also alerts farmers to any potential leaks, ensuring that water is not wasted due to faulty pipes or equipment. Our solution is designed for ease of use and flexibility, offering farmers the ability to monitor their irrigation system remotely, all through a simple mobile or web interface. With no complex automation required, it provides a sustainable, affordable, and scalable solution to help farmers manage their irrigation more efficiently, even in areas with limited access to technology. This system is an ideal choice for small to medium-sized farms looking to optimize water usage while promoting sustainability and reducing operational costs.
